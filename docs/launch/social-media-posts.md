## Creative Direction Notes

### Visual Identity for Social Posts
- **Color palette:** Warm earth tones from the app (the brown/cream from the UI), paired with dark backgrounds for contrast
- **Typography:** Mix of serif (for emotional/nostalgic lines) and mono/sans-serif (for tech credibility)
- **Screenshots:** Use the actual app screenshots, but frame them in a device mockup (MacBook or iPhone showing the desktop app)

### What Makes People Stop Scrolling
1. **The paradox** — "I texted my 22-year-old self. He replied." — This creates cognitive dissonance. People HAVE to find out how.
2. **The specificity** — Don't say "AI-powered nostalgia tool." Say "He remembered a hackathon project I'd forgotten about."
3. **The privacy flip** — In a world where every AI app wants your data, "works with Wi-Fi off" is a genuine pattern interrupt.

### CTA Strategy
- **Instagram:** "Link in bio" + "What would you ask your younger self?" (drives comments)
- **Facebook:** Direct link + "Drop your question in the comments" (drives engagement + reshares)
- **WhatsApp:** Direct link + "Lmk what your younger self tells you" (drives replies)

### Hashtag Strategy (Instagram)
**Primary (high-intent):** #ai #opensource #privacy #localai #buildinpublic
**Emotional (discovery):** #nostalgia #selfreflection #personalgrowth #timecapsule
**Platform (reach):** #whatsapp #chathistory #sidproject #indiedev
