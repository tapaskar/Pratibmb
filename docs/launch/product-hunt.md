## Product Name
Pratibmb

## Tagline (60 chars max)
Chat with your 10-years-younger self. 100% local.

## Description

Pratibmb imports your messaging history from WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram, Gmail, iMessage, Telegram, Twitter/X, and Discord — then lets you text past-you.

A local AI learns your voice, your relationships, and your life events. Pick a year, ask a question, and your past self responds in your actual texting style. Optional LoRA fine-tuning makes it even more authentic.

Everything runs on your machine. No cloud. No API keys. No telemetry. No accounts. After the initial model download, it works with Wi-Fi off.

Free for personal use. Open source (AGPLv3).

## Maker Comment

I built Pratibmb because I wanted to explore my own messaging history with AI — but I wasn't willing to upload a decade of private messages to anyone's cloud.

The result: a fully local RAG + fine-tuning pipeline that runs on a regular laptop. No GPU required for chat (optional for fine-tuning on Windows/Linux). Apple Silicon Macs get GPU-accelerated fine-tuning out of the box via MLX.

What surprised me most: how differently I texted in 2014. The model picks up on patterns you've forgotten — phrases you used, topics you cared about, even your sentence structure.

Happy to answer any questions about the architecture or approach!
