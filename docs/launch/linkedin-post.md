I just open-sourced something I've been building for months.

Pratibmb lets you chat with your past self — using your real messaging history.

Import your WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram, Gmail, iMessage, Telegram, Twitter, or Discord chats. A local AI learns your voice and lets you text the person you were 5, 10, even 15 years ago.

Why build this?

Because the most intimate data you own — a decade of private messages — is the one dataset you should never upload to somebody else's API.

Every "AI assistant" service requires sending your data to the cloud. Pratibmb runs entirely on your machine. No cloud. No API keys. No telemetry. After the initial model download, it works with Wi-Fi off.

What surprised me the most: how differently I texted in 2014. More exclamation marks, more emoji, completely different slang. The model picked up on patterns I'd genuinely forgotten about.

It's free for personal use, open source (AGPLv3), and available for macOS, Linux, and Windows.

Website: https://pratibmb.com
GitHub: https://github.com/tapaskar/Pratibmb

If you've ever wondered what you'd say to your younger self — now you can actually try it.
